Transforming Talent Management Experience with Job Market Data

Introduction:

We are dathusiats733 and here in this final project we have the passion and objectives to transform the way talent management experience with job market data by providing:

  • Data Driven Talent Management approach

  • Model to provide insights in facilitating HR decisions

Questions we would like to answer in this project:

  1. Is there a clear relationship between the job market status quo and employee retention? Are those employees with skills in high demand less likely to stay put with company?
  2. What can we do preemptively to make them stay? What are the key factors affecting the willingness of employees staying with the company?
  3. Are salary / short-term incentives useful in increasing employee satisfaction?

Our Datasets Used:

We analyzed on the job market data on modeling influence by external factors - mainly salary and job demand

  • External datasets on the job postings during the period of investigation from major job platforms
    • Indeed
    • LinkedIn
    • Workopolis
    • Glassdoor
  • The census from Statistics Canada about occupations

Regarding employee data, we chose IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition and Performance dataset, which is synthetic in nature, for our employee retention analysis.

Our Methodology:

1. Data Collection

There are 3 different datasets collected for our project.
  • Latest job posts scraped from the four major job search platforms (Indeed, LinkedIn, Workopolis, Glassdoor)
  • Average Salary information for respective job titles in Canada is scraped from Talent
  • IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition and Performance dataset obtained from Kaggle
Employee dataset can be replaced by the company's own employee dataset that is lookind adapt this solution.
 
2. Data Pre-processing and Integration
 
We pre-processed job posts collected in different forms and integrate all the job posts as a single data source for further analysis
  • Remove duplicate job posts within respective datasets
  • Assign unique jobpost_id to those records whose ids are not available
  • Jaccard Similarity: Apply Jaccard Similarity to detect and remove similar (possibly the same) job posts collected from different platforms
3. Data Cleansing and Standardization
 
In order to facilitate easier data handilng and EDA (which will be conducted later in the pipeline), we performed data cleaning and standardize values. Standardizaiton of values includes: expected salary, location, remote work indicators.
 
4. Data Storage
 
We have used MongoDB to store all the data we have collected for our anlaysis.
 
5. Data Analysis
 
We performed a wide range of data analysis to derive meaninful insights from the available data.
  • Job Skill Extraction: Using NLP, we are extracting sought-after skillsets for different job titles
  • Training of machine learning model: Using the avialble datasets, implement a machine learning model that will predict probabilities of a particular employee leaving the company
  • EDA: Identify interesting patterns and trend in the current job market
6. Data Visualization
 
Using this web front-end interface, we displayed all the interesting facts and predictions:
  • Visualization of EDA
  • Employee Retention Prediction
